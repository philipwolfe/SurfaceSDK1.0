This Microsoft Surface unit includes the Windows Vista® operating system and the Microsoft Surface software. The Microsoft Surface software enables you to run touch-enabled applications and turn an ordinary tabletop into a vibrant, interactive surface where users can interact with digital content through natural gestures, touch, and physical objects.

If this unit is a Microsoft Surface developer unit, it also includes the Microsoft Surface SDK, which includes the tools and documentation that you need to develop applications for Microsoft Surface. To learn more about the Microsoft Surface SDK, click Start, click All Programs, click Microsoft Surface SDK 1.0, and then click Surface SDK Documentation.

Microsoft Surface Resources

This Microsoft Surface unit includes the printed Microsoft Surface Operation and Maintenance Guide that includes important safety and maintenance information.

You can also find the Microsoft Surface documentation and support on the Microsoft Surface Community Web site.

Note: If you are not registered for the Surface Community Web site, please contact the person in your company who is responsible for requesting Surface Community accounts or e-mail us directly at partners@surface.com and include your e-mail address, the name of your company, and your title or role (for example, developer or IT administrator).

The Microsoft Surface Community Web site includes the latest information, downloads, and forums for everyone in your company who is working with Microsoft Surface, including resources for developers, designers, IT administrators, and many other roles. In particular, the site includes the following information plus many more articles and downloads:

  • Learning the Surface Basics: A series of training videos to introduce you to Microsoft Surface.
  • Microsoft Surface Administration Guide: How to administer, maintain, deploy, and troubleshoot Microsoft Surface units in your office or venue.
  • Calibrating a Surface Unit: How and when to run basic and full calibration on a Microsoft Surface unit by using the Calibrate Vision System tool.
  • Ambient Light Indicator Tool: How and when to use the Ambient Light Indicator tool to check the lighting around a Microsoft Surface unit.
  • Sample Applications: An overview of the applications that are available from Microsoft®.
